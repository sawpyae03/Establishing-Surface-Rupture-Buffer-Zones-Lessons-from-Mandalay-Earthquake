# Establishing Surface Rupture Buffer Zones  
## Lessons from the 2025 M7.7 Mandalay Earthquake and the Sagaing Fault Corridor

This repository contains Geographic Information System (GIS) datasets for the proposed **Seismic Buffer Zones (SBZ)** along the ~500 km rupture corridor of the **March 28, 2025, M7.7 Mandalay Earthquake**.  

The research proposes a **three-tier zoning system** to mitigate risks from primary surface rupture and the expanded **Coseismic Damage Zone (CDZ)** associated with supershear rupture propagation.

# Technical Specifications

- **Coordinate Reference System (CRS):** EPSG:4326 — WGS 84  
- **Methodology:** The **300 ft Yellow Zone** is calibrated to the ~100 m **Coseismic Damage Zone (CDZ)** width identified by Wei et al. (2025).  
- **Geometric Baseline:** Buffer distances are generated from a digitized **single-trace centerline** to ensure regulatory clarity for planners and surveyors.

---

# Regulatory Framework

Based on the integrated **risk matrix** developed in the accompanying research paper, land-use permissibility depends on the interaction between **hazard zones** and **Myanmar National Building Code (MNBC 2025) Occupancy Categories**.

---

## 1. New Construction

### 🔴 Red Zone (0–50 ft)
- Deterministic avoidance zone.
- Only **low-risk, non-habitable Category I assets** (e.g., agricultural sheds) are permitted.

### 🟡 Yellow Zone (50–300 ft)
- **Category II buildings are restricted.**
- Approval requires **site-specific paleoseismic trenching or geophysical investigations** to confirm the absence of secondary fault shears.

### 🔵 Blue Zone (300 ft – 0.5 mile)
- **Essential facilities (Category III & IV) are restricted** to maintain resilience against **near-fault seismic effects**.

---

## 2. Existing Buildings

- Mandatory **seismic assessment and retrofitting** requirements depend on:
  - Hazard Zone
  - Occupancy Category
  - Post-Disaster Rapid Assessment (**PDRA**) safety tags  
    - Green  
    - Yellow  
    - Red

- Within the **Red Zone**, seismic assessments are **mandatory for Category III and IV structures regardless of tag color**.
